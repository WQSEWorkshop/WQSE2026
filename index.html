About the Workshop

Quantum computing is rapidly moving from theoretical physics to practical software engineering. The I Brazilian Workshop on Quantum Software Engineering (WQSE) aims to bring together researchers, practitioners, and educators to discuss the challenges and opportunities in building robust, scalable, and maintainable quantum software systems.
